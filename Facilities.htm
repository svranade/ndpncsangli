College Facilities :  
                 The College provide following facilities to the students.      
 

(a)          Our Girls are accommodated in the following hostels run by the Latthe Education society.
I.                   Sou. Hausabai Keshavrao Chougule hostel for girls.
II.                  K. W. College hostel for Girls.
 III.                LES day care centre for working women.

(b)          Sangli is an educationally developed city, it has many boardings and hostels such as
(a)                 Lingayat Boarding
(b)                 Jain Boarding
(c)                  Gandhi Boarding
(d)                 Government's Dr. Ambedkar Hostel.
Most of the students are from near-by areas.  Those coming from far off areas are admitted in the above hostels on the recommendation of the college.

 
 

The students of the college can avail of the following financial aids.

a)       National Scholarship

b)       Government of India Scholarship to scheduled castes and tribes and     lower income groups.

c)       Scholarship to children of primary and secondary school teachers.

d)       Maharashtra state non-backward class students open merit freeship.

e)       Maharashtra state economically backward class free studentship.

f)        Freeship to sons and daughters of primary teachers in Maharashtra     state.

g)       Freeship to Scheduled Caste and Schedule Tribe students.

 
 

                   Our College provides the following prizes:

      a)       Ad. Nanasaheb Nandrekar Ardhamagadhi Award.
                            This award is given to the student standing first in Ardhamagadhi                             subject at B.A.I. & II.
                  b)       Shivgonda Anna Patil Award
                            This award is given to the students standing first in B.A.Part I, II, III                             and B.com Part I, II, III.
                 c)        Shri. Vasant Mahadev Jadhav Prize Merit
                            This Award is given to the student standing first in Sociology at                             T.Y.B.A.

 
 

                   Our College provides the following Facilities in Sports :
            a)         We have a common auditorium with the facilities of courts  for                                 Badminton, Table tennis, Weight lifting, Boxing and Chess and Carom.
          b)         Play-fields - Playground at measuring 80 m x 65 m with a open track                      and the grounds 25 m x 15 m for Kho-Kho, Kabaddi, Volleyball,                       Handball, Basketball, Soft ball, Cricket net and athletics.
