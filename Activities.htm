Activities :  
 
a) Our N.S.S. students took part in Blood-donation camp organised by the Government Civil     Hospital, Sangli and also the Shirgaonkar Blood-Bank, Sangli.

b) Our students and our teachers participated and filled up the forms of Eye-Donation,     organised by National Association for Blind (NAB) and Paranjape Eye-Bank, Sangli.

c) Our teachers and students took part in the activities organised by Rotary Innerwheel     Club Sangli, especially medical check up camp and Blood donation camp.

d) Our N.S.S. Volunteers actively participated in the cleanliness drive at Bhide Remand     Home, Sangli, Hutatma Smarak, Haripur, Bapat Mala, (Mahavir Udyan) Sangli.

e) The college allows the students and teachers to participate in the activities such as     'Sadbhawna Rally'.

f) The college takes part by allowing the students to participate in the Pulse-Polio     programme.

g) Students take part in the social functions such as Sahitya Samhelan organised by     Student's Association.

h) We encourage the students to take part in the anti-social activity drives such as anti-    terrorism campaign and anti-dowry drive, anti-addiction drive. Many times our     college     gives monitory help to the enthusiastic students eager to take part in such     activities.

i) The college allows the teachers to take part in such seminars, conferences, workshops    and symposia.
