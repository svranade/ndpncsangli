About College :

           Education makes a man knowledgeable and makes him aware of values of life. But it is through higher education that the internal and external development of his personality takes place. The majority of the students learning in the day college, are able to take advantage of this kind of education. However, there are a number of students who are deprived of the opportunity of higher education only because they have to earn their bread and maintain their family by employing themselves and spending their energy in doing sundry jobs.
 
            With the noble aim of providing a golden opportunity of higher education to needy students and enable them to complete their education and enable them to lead their life on par with the other students with dignity in the modern competitive world, Latthe Education Society started the night college on non-grant basis in the building of Smt. K.W. College on 15th June 1983. In other words the society opened the doors of higher education to those needy students who were struggling to get an education on the basis of earn and learn policy.
            At the beginning, the number of students was limited to 57, but today there are as many as one thousand students who are availing themselves of this college. At the initial stage the specializations in English, History and Political Science were introduce under arts faculty. Whereas, special subjects like Accountancy and Banking were introduce under commerce faculty. At present, provision has been made for teaching subjects such as Marathi, Hindi and Sociology. Consequently, three-year integrated courses in B.A. and B.Com are being run in our college. Similarly, this college is affiliated to Shivaji University.
            As the college was established through the glorious sacrifice of Nemgonda Dada Patil, it was named as Nemgonda Dada Patil Night College of Arts and Commerce, Sangli. The ceremony took place on 12th January 1990 at the auspicious hands of Dr.P.B.Sable, the then registrar of Shivaji University, and present vice chancellor of Yashwantrao Chavan open university, Nashik.
            Our college has a team of duly qualified, young and talented teachers. This has considerably helped in providing quality education. Two members of the teaching staff have Ph.D. and six have M.Phil. degrees and four of them are doing Ph.D. in their respective subjects.
            With such bright galaxy of teachers, our college is in a position to provide academic programmes. Two students of our college Mr. Mhetre Sachin Ganpatrao and Patil Appaso Dada have been rewarded with university merit scholarships.
            In order to help the deserving and an economically backward students, the Students' Aid Fund has been started. From this fund financial assistance is provided to the economically disadvantaged students. There is a book bank facility for such students.
            In the academic year 2000-2001 and 2001-2002 the performance of the B.A.III and B.Com. III students of the university examinations has been quite satisfactory. In arts faculty thirteen students have secured 1st class.
            In Gymkhana and Athletics our performance has been commendable, almost from the beginning and especially from 1992. in the year 1992, Ms. Sangeeta Kulkarni stood 13th in Pune Internation Marathon and was selected for the Republic Day Parade. In the year 1996-1997, Shri. Ganapati Bhunur participated in the International invitation Cross-Country Tournament held at Ciba (Japan) and Italy. Our Kabaddi men's team was the winner of Zonal and runner up of Inter-Zonal Kabaddi tournament (2000-2001). Ms. Sarita Patil won the General Championship in Zonal and Inter-Zonal Athletics Meet. She was the best women Athlete in the 5th inter set, inter university Ashwamedh tournament held at Shivaji University. She a winner of Silver Medal in Inter-University Athletics meet in High-Jump and a Gold Medalist in Senior Nationals and in federation cup athletics meet in High-Jump.
 
GOALS AND MISSION OF THE INSTITUTION & COLLEGE :  
1. To impart qualitative and valuable service in the field of education to the nearby areas in     general, and Sangli in particular.
2. To attain community and social development through infra-structural facilities of the     institution.
3. To ensure and inculcate perfect discipline in terms of regularity, sincerity and punctuality     amongst the students so that they contribute to the society and nation as most responsible     and respectable citizens.
4. To aim at overall personality development of the students fraternity through extra-curricular     activities in association with various social and cultural organization.
5. To provide a platform to the students, by giving them an opportunity to face all the     challenges of the competitive world, with utmost utilisation of their potential in sports,     athletics & other events.
6. To provide educational facilities in Higher education to the different working/ employed     students from the poor sections of the society.
7. To educate and train the students to self-help and prepare them for self-employment.
