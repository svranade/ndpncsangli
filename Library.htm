Library :
           There is an advisory committee for the library consisting of the principal and the Heads of various departments.
           The following activities are undertaken.
 
 
                    a) Allocation of budget to different departments.
                    b) Selection of books by HODs.
                    c) The recommended reference books by the concerned teachers are                         purchased.
                    d) Appropriate utilization of funds.
           Including our college library, there are five libraries of sister colleges on the campus such as the libraries of K.W. College, N. S. Law College, G. A. College of Commerce and Kasturba Junior College of Education. All these libraries are inter connected for the purpose of inter-library borrowing. The Latthe Education Society has provided a computer for the inter-library borrowings of these colleges. It is centrally located in K.W. College. In addition to this we (teachers and students) make use of the university (PG) library managed by Smt. K. W. College.
 
 
Working days of the library :-
          The library is kept open for all working days of the college except Sundays and holidays.
Working hours of the library :-
          2.00 p.m. to 9.30 p.m.
          During examination the reading hall is kept open for study purpose upto 10.00 p.m. The management has made provision of another reading hall for 24 hours in the campus for the students during the examination.
