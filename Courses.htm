## ARTS  DEPARTMENT ##
 		  
  B.A. Part - I  
 
          Candidate admitting to this course is required to face 6 papers evaluating 100 marks each from which 2 are compulsory and 4 are elective.
          The candidate is availed following subjects.
  
  Compulsory Subjects -
1) English
2) Marathi 		 
  Optional Subjects -  
  1) Marathi Or Economics
2) English Or Sociology
3) Hindi Or Physical Education
4) Ardhamagadhi Or Political Science
5) History Or Psychology		  
  Note : Out of 5 groups stated above candidate has to select 4 elective subjects which must comprise one language and one sociological subject.  
  B.A. Part - II  
 

          Candidate admitting to this course is required to face 6 papers evaluating 100 marks each. 2 subjects are compulsory and 2 are elective (each with 2 papers).
          The candidate is availed following subjects.

 
  Compulsory Subjects -
1) English
2) Select One Subject from given below subjects which is related to Optional Subjects in     B.A. Part II.

  B.A. Part - III  
 

         Candidate admitting to this course is required to face 6 papers evaluating 100 marks each.
         In this course English will be the compulsory subject and there are 5 papers for the special subject selected. Candidate can choose the special subject from the elective subjects selected in B.A. Part II only.
          The candidate is availed following subjects.

 
       1) Marathi
     2) English
     3) Hindi
     4) History
     5) Pol. Science
     6) Sociology		  
 
## COMMERCE  DEPARTMENT ##
 		  
 

        This section offers courses up to graduation. Candidate passes through 3 examinations namely B.Com I, B.Com II, B.Com III in three years respectively which are conducted by Shivaji University.

 
  B.Com Part I  
 

        Candidate admitting to this course is required to face 6 papers evaluating 100 marks. The candidate is availed following subjects.

 
       1) English Paper I
     2) Marathi OR Principles of Marketing
     3) Insurance
     4) Business Economics Paper I
     5) Principles Management of Marketing
     6) Accountancy		  
  B.Com Part II  
 

        Candidate admitting to this course is required to face 6 papers evaluating 100 marks. The candidate is availed following subjects.

 
       1) English Paper II
     2) Business Economics Paper II
     3) Banking and Finance
     4) Statistics
     5) Business Planning and Polices		  
  B.Com Part III  
 

        Candidate admitting to this course is required to face 6 papers evaluating 100 marks. The candidate is availed following subjects.

 
       1) World Economic Environment
     2) Co-operative Development
     3) Management Thought
     4) Business Laws
     5) From below elective subjects one subject is of 2 papers
         a) Advanced Accounting Paper I
         b) Advanced Accounting Paper II
                     OR
         a) Advanced Banking  Paper I
         b) Advanced Banking  Paper II
