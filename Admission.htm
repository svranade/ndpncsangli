Admission :

B.A. Part I :    
       Any one satisfying any of the following conditions is eligible to get admission for B.A. Part - I.
1) One who has passed 12th Standard examination from Maharashtra State Higher     Education Board wherein subjects include English, four Optional subjects and any other     language. OR
2) One who has passed 12th Standard examination from Maharashtra State Higher     Education Board wherein subjects include English, any three subjects from Arts side     and one Vocational Subject of 200 marks. OR
3) One who has passed any other Examination from any University or Educational Borad,     and Shivaji University considers the exam to be equivalent .
 
 
B.A. Part II :
          A candidate is eligible to get admission for B.A. Part -II if he has passed B.A. part - I examination from Shivaji University or examination from other University provided Shivaji University considers the examination to be equivqlent.
          Also student getting A.T.K.T. for B.A. Part-I examination from Shivaji University is also eligible.
B.A. Part III :
          A candidate is eligible to get admission for B.A. Part -III if he has passed B.A. part - II examination from Shivaji University or examination from other University provided Shivaji University considers the examination to be equivqlent.
          Also student getting A.T.K.T. for B.A. Part-II examination from Shivaji University is also eligible.

Regarding Admissions for the candidate from other Universities :
A) A candidate from other college belonging to Shivaji University needs to provide Transference Certificate for admission. Admission for such candidate will be considered provisional until he / she presents the same.
B) A candidate belonging to any other Universities from Maharashtra or a candidate from from any other State is required to present following documents.
       1) Migration Certificate
       2) Transference Certificate
       3) Eligibility Certificate
Satisfying above conditions in prescribed time is personal responsibility of the candidate.
  
 
For Foreign Candidate :
At the time of admission in addition to Migration, Transference and Eligibility Certificates every foreign candidate will have to produce the certificate of permission issued by Govt. of India.
The children of NRIs who have been residing, working abroad for more than two years and seeking admission in this college will have to undergo AIDS test and the admission will be confirmed only after production fitness certificate.
